<objective>
Fix two confirmation-flow inconsistencies: (1) the TUI rebuilds a brand-new rich.live.Live on every confirm resume, leaving duplicate stale panels and skipped step numbers; and (2) config sync prompts interactively even under --dry-run, unlike the orchestrator's other confirmations which skip prompting in a rehearsal.

Purpose: TerminalUI.start() always constructs a fresh Live (transient=False), and Confirmer/config_sync call stop()+start() around every prompt, so each confirmation leaves the prior frame in scrollback and starts a new region below it. Reusing a single Live across pause/resume keeps the display and step counter continuous. Separately, _prompt_new_config prompts unconditionally under --dry-run while _confirm_first_sync skips the question in dry-run — aligning them removes a confusing "Apply this config? [y/n]" during a read-only rehearsal. Closes UAT gaps 3 and 5.

Output: A pausable single-instance TUI, confirmer/config_sync using pause/resume, dry-run-consistent config sync, and tests.
</objective>
