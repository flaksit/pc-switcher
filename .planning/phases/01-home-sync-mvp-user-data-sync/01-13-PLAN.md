<objective>
Replace the two old sync-ordering/divergence overrides with a single topology-based safety step. Remove the pre-phase consecutive-sync check and all allow_divergence plumbing; add a new "out-of-order / target-state" step that runs after the target lock, reads both machines' sync-history (last_role + last_peer), and warns+confirms only when the target may hold independent state. Record last_peer on both ends. Consolidate the CLI onto --allow-out-of-order.

Purpose: This is the human reflection checkpoint that replaces the removed btrfs content guard (ADR-015). It must not hard-block the legit back-and-forth (#159), must clearly flag the machine-C and no-history cases, and must stay non-interactive-safe.

Output: Orchestrator, CLI, and JobContext wired to the topology model, with reworked tests.
</objective>
